About AMRBH25 LLC

Leading the future of metabolic health research and precision nutrition

Our Mission

AMRBH25 LLC is a US-based clinical research and consulting company established in Florida, dedicated to advancing the field of metabolic health through innovative research, precision nutrition protocols, and evidence-based therapeutic solutions. We specialize in obesity management, metabolic syndrome treatment, and the development of personalized nutrition interventions that transform patient outcomes.

Scientific Excellence

Our commitment to scientific innovation drives everything we do. We employ rigorous research methodologies, multidisciplinary collaboration, and evidence-based protocols to ensure the highest standards of clinical research. Our team works closely with medical clinics, healthcare professionals, and research institutions to translate cutting-edge science into practical, effective solutions for metabolic health challenges.

Regulatory Compliance

AMRBH25 LLC operates in full compliance with all applicable federal and state regulations, including FDA guidelines, HIPAA requirements, and Florida state health regulations. Our commitment to regulatory excellence ensures that all research activities and protocol implementations meet the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and ethical conduct.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for metabolic health and clinical research

Clinical Research Coordination

Design, oversight, and management of multicenter clinical studies focusing on metabolic health, GLP-1 receptor agonists (including Semaglutide and Tirzepatide), and related therapeutic interventions. Our comprehensive approach ensures rigorous study design, ethical conduct, and meaningful outcomes.

Precision Nutrition Protocols

Development and implementation of individualized nutrition and lifestyle protocols utilizing advanced anthropometry (ISAK methodology) and genetic data analysis. Our precision approach ensures personalized interventions that maximize therapeutic outcomes while minimizing adverse effects.

Professional Training

Comprehensive training and supervision programs for healthcare professionals, including physicians, physician assistants, and health coaches. Our curriculum covers best practices in metabolic monitoring, nutrition assessment, adverse event management, and patient engagement strategies.

Digital Health Solutions

Creation and deployment of innovative digital tools including mobile applications, patient dashboards, and data collection platforms. These solutions enhance patient engagement, streamline data collection, and provide real-time progress tracking for weight-loss and metabolic health programs.

Scientific Communication

Publication of research findings in peer-reviewed journals, preparation of educational materials for healthcare professionals, and representation at national and international scientific conferences. We bridge the gap between research and clinical practice through effective scientific communication.

Regulatory & Compliance Consulting

Expert guidance on FDA regulations, HIPAA compliance, and state health regulations to ensure full regulatory compliance for all research and protocol activities. Our consulting services help organizations navigate complex regulatory landscapes while maintaining the highest standards of patient safety and data integrity.
